Methodology


Most of our engagements are for a fixed price. This is possible because experience tells us exactly what it takes to solve problem.
The average implementation is complete in twelve weeks or less and users experience a 30-40% boost in productivity. We can replicate these results because we follow a three phase methodology:


Process
Analysis

We work with you to understand and tighten your business workflows using our tested methodology. Quickly and easily, we'll effectively capture how technology can help your team best and show you where you can save significant amounts of money.

Data
Mapping

We examine the information going in and listen to what is required to come from the system. We ask difficult question, bring different data sets together or help recover lost or corrupted data from a previous system or breach. Then we map the delta to ensure you can actually get from point A to point B.

Technology
Implementation

We work with your existing vendors and technical team, supplementing with other expertise needed and our relationships with the technology brand team. We work together to configure your system for your unique requirements, including showing you what the technology can do for your business process so you can maximize your investment.

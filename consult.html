Process
Optimization

We start by documenting what is actually happening in the business process you feel the pain, either Sales, Marketing, Finance, Product/Service Delivery, Human Resource Management, Legal, Risk or Compliance. Quickly and easily, you can see where your team can minimize cost and maximizing throughput and/or efficiency. It's a baselining step that gets everyone in the same understanding of what is occurring in the company today and where can harness the most gains with technology. This step is quick and after we're complete, executives are thankful we went through it because of how much they learned AND how much they realized they could have lost by implementing technology without knowing this first.

Data
Analysis

We collect all the data sets you already have and those you didn't know you had. We work with you to get the data ready for the new system, whether that means creating a new numbering system for parts and re-tagging all the existing information, or brining very different data sets together or recovering lost or corrupted data from a previous system or a breach. We identify the type of reporting you want to achieve at the end of implementation and determine all the processing steps in the middle necessary to get the complex reporting and analysis you require for data driven business decisions as automatically as possible.

Technology
Implementation

After everyone is clear how you're doing business and what data needs to come into the system and how, we work with your team to create a functional specification for what new system needs to be implemented, how the data will flow through the company (either through integrations or through people in the workflow), if systems need to be redeployed or if any custom development is required. We will help you understand what the technology can do for your business process so you get the most use out of your investment, configure it, set up the reporting, train the users and manage any technical vendors required before turning over the systems back to you, ready to use.

Our Services

System Implementation

You need a new system, to expand your current system, to transition out of a legacy system or to merge your system with another. We understand what your goals are, what resources you already have in place and what already exists in your company. We examine your work flow, collect your data and help you select the best technology on the market for your specific need. Our team configures your system, brings in your data, completes the integrations, and formats the reports to your liking. We train your team on using the new system and processes and leave you with the resources you need to manage it on your own.

System Optimization

You have a system with data in it and it works fine or you don’t have the option to change it. We can help make using that system more effective and efficient. Some examples include: 1) transition to electronic workflows and processes - the paperless and paperlight offices. This includes support of Electronic Medical Record conversions. 2) integration of systems or combination of data sets for reporting 3) any complex dashboarding and analytics, especially if you can’t get or find the data in your existing technology. We identify where you’d like to be leaner or faster, work with or train your team to do the work if you prefer, provide business analysis or setup the systems, queries and reports that you need.

Our Team

What if those responsible for making sure the company succeeds were also the people who understood how to build the tools to house a company's most valuable asset - Data -?
As one client likes to say, it's what makes our analysts "clarivoyant"."

Technologists

OOur technologists understand how the technology itself works, how it behaves in a local or cloud based network environment and when integrated to other systems. They know the pros and cons of leading brands and those emerging. When what you need doesn't exist, they can help get it built.

Business Analysts

Our analysts are trained profiteers with methodologies to determine the most efficient and effective way the businesses, departments and processes should be organized, re-engineered or designed. They perform initial project assessments, provide user training and help with any configuration that requires automation of a workflow.

Data Analysts

Our data analysts understand data and the human behavior around it. They understand the regulations in your industry and help ensure data security through integrations. They have expertise in key performance indicators, dashboarding and analysis driving decision making and have established wizardry in query and report creation.
